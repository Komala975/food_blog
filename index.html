Biryani Recipe

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with basmati rice, spices, and your choice of meat or vegetables. Here's a simple recipe:

  • Ingredients: Basmati rice, chicken, yogurt, onions, tomatoes, spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala), saffron, and herbs.
  • Steps: Marinate the chicken, prepare the rice, layer the ingredients, and cook on low heat for perfect biryani.

Cold Coffee Recipe

Cold coffee is a refreshing beverage perfect for hot days. Here's how to make it:

  • Ingredients: Instant coffee, milk, sugar, ice cubes, and chocolate syrup (optional).
  • Steps: Blend all the ingredients until frothy, pour into a glass, and garnish with chocolate syrup or whipped cream.

Pizza Recipe

Homemade pizza is both fun and delicious. Here's a basic recipe:

  • Ingredients: Pizza dough, tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings (pepperoni, bell peppers, olives).
  • Steps: Spread sauce on the dough, add cheese and toppings, and bake at 220°C (425°F) for 15-20 minutes.
